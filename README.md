# AnalystLab Africa Data Analytics Internship

## Week 1–2: Data Cleaning & Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)

### Project Overview

This project was completed as part of the AnalystLab Africa Data Analytics Internship Program (Batch B 2026).

The objective was to clean, validate, and analyze two real-world datasets:

1. Online Retail Dataset
2. Netflix Movies & TV Shows Dataset

The project focused on transforming raw data into analysis-ready datasets while uncovering meaningful patterns and trends through exploratory data analysis (EDA).

## Key Insights

### Online Retail

* The United Kingdom generated the highest revenue.
* Revenue increased significantly during the final months of the year.
* A small number of products generated a large share of total revenue.
* Customer spending was concentrated among a small number of customers.
* Revenue distribution was highly right-skewed.

### Netflix

* Movies dominate Netflix's content library.
* Content additions increased substantially over time.
* The United States contributed the largest amount of content.
* TV-MA and TV-14 were among the most common ratings.
* Popular genres accounted for a significant share of available content.
